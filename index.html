About us

Phonesackgroup Company Ltd.,

Phonesack Group (PSG) is registered and head quartered in Vientiane, the Capital city of Lao PDR.

We are wholly owned Lao companies with multiple sister companies and subsidiaries. Our long history of doing business in Lao PDR has allowed us to diversify into multiple industries and gain expertise in a wide range of fields.

We employ over 2000 employees across all sites with numerous sub-contractors contributing to the overall economic and social development of Lao PDR.
